OUR CORE VALUES

Admin / 20 March 2015

At Fresh Burger™, we are committed to serving our customers the best burgers at the best price possible. Fresh is not just a buzzword we use in our restaurants, nor do we place efficiency before the quality of our food. To us, fresh means fresh meat, fresh vegetables, and fresh bread, which is why you will not find any freezers in our restaurants. We use the highest-grade beef (100% Canadian Angus Beef) that is ground daily to our specifications, which is why Fresh Burger™ has earned its reputation as the Premium burger brand in the Greater Toronto Area. For as long as Fresh Burger™ exists, we promise to serve the best tasting burgers and fries in the market at the best possible prices — every single time.

OUR STORY

Admin / 16 April 2004

Fresh Burger™ opened its first doors on September 3, 2013 in Richmond Hill, Ontario. It quickly grew into a neighbourhood hot spot, with line-ups out the door during the lunchtime rush. The success followed Fresh Burger™ to Toronto’s Church Street with the opening of the second store in November 2014. Fresh Burger™ continued to expand, opening in Vaughan in February 2018 and then North York in September 2018. The Richmond Hill location was expanded to double its original size in February 2016. This was followed by a second North York store on Yonge Street in August 2019, Airport Road in Mississauga in October 2019 and Dixie Road, also in Mississauga, in November 2019.
