About Us

What Makes Our Taste?

Fresh Burger™ was born in 2013 after a fruitless search for a good old-fashioned hamburger. During his days in the financial industry, Jacques traveled the world and discovered that the best food, anywhere in the world, could be found in restaurants that had a limited menu.

. With the hamburger being one of his favourite foods, Jacques frequented many hamburger spots during his travels. Back home in Toronto, the inability to find a well-made classic hamburger was particularly frustrating to him.
